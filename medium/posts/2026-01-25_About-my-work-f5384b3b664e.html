About my work

I write because some things refuse to stay unexamined.

My work lives at the intersection of structure and feeling. Some days that means essays shaped by law and research, some days it means reviews, culture, media, or long-form reflections that linger a little longer than expected.

I’ve been writing professionally since 2019, working with clients on brand stories, reviews, and editorial-style pieces. I took a step back between 2023–2025 to focus on my legal career, and I’m now returning to writing with more clarity, patience, and intent.

What you’ll find across my work is not noise, but thought. Writing that values tone, pacing, and readability. Writing that trusts the reader.

If you’re looking for polished but human writing — the kind that doesn’t rush to impress — this is probably the right place.
