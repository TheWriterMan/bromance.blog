Why Should You Consult A Lawyer?

First, let’s talk about the problem here — the reason you need to check whether hiring a lawyer would be of any use to you.

  • Reason one could be lawyers are expensive.
  • Reason two could be that you are wary of hiring a lawyer because of all the bad stories you have heard.
  • Reason three could be that you believe you don’t really need one at the moment.

I understand these reasons. And they are justified.

As a lawyer myself, I understand how difficult is to find a good lawyer who won’t exploit you. My lawyer-reason here is not to counter any of your non-lawyer reasons, of course.

However (generally) people seek a lawyer when they are already in a state of panic and don’t understand what they’re supposed to do. What people need to understand is that consulting a lawyer is crucial so, whether you want to or not is out of the question.

According to Victoria Pynchon, “It’s okay to hate lawyers”. And since the dawn of time, it is one of the most despised professions.

“The first thing we do, let’s kill all the lawyers.”

— William Shakespeare, Henry VI, Part 2

Well, that would have been great if we didn’t really need lawyers. But we NEED them especially when we live in a society that runs on rules and regulations (thankfully, of course. We wouldn’t like to be in a purge-like situation.)

I am sure you’ve seen a movie or two in which Tom Cruise or Denzel Washington, personified as a lawyer, saves the day. Although glorified, to a certain extent, saving lives is what they do. So, in the case you’re served a notice, it is better to consult a lawyer instead of waiting. For all you know, you’re wasting precious time.

The number one reason you should consult a lawyer is that you are a non-lawyer, someone who may not completely be able to understand what you have gotten yourself into or how to get out of it.

Legal proceedings take a lot of time. So, you might want to begin as soon as possible. Consulting a lawyer would be the ideal step as they look for best alternatives that could save your precious time.

For example, in the case of a criminal suit, you might want to consult a lawyer straightaway. Hesitating for long could cost you more than you think.

Talking to a lawyer is expensive but not always. There are plenty of great lawyers who ‘do the job’ just as efficiently as any high-priced lawyer out there. You don’t always need to look for a “famous” lawyer.

You may have heard a story from someone about their bad experience with a lawyer. And I agree that there are some not-so-good lawyers out there but that is the case with every profession. For instance, just because we had a bad hospital experience, it’s not like we never visit the hospital when we’re sick. We do but we go to a different one.

In the end, you need to understand that lawyers are humans too (just a tad bit different). Their profession takes a toll on their mental and physical health and that is the reason they charge a lot so that after the case, they can go and have therapy, and pay for their spouse’s therapy as well.
