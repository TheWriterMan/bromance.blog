[According to the Content Marketing Institute,](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/2016/08/content-marketing-stats/)**73% of companies hire copywriters**or someone to write content for them. So, what does this data suggest?

To Hire A Copywriter? Well, no.

You can be creative and write everything on your own but the question is: will it be effective? Will it be able to do what it’s supposed to do? The data suggests that 73% of companies hire copywriters because they are able to increase their ROI. They are able to deliver results. This is because copywriters have the skill and expertise required to write good copy.

### Copywriting is the art of innovating, informing, and inspiring readers. A copy is a powerful tool that motivates readers to take a specific action. Artists who create copy are known as copywriters and good ones are capable of selling anything and everything. It is undoubtedly one of the best marketing tools in the market today.

![It is what it is.](https://res.cloudinary.com/dtperak4e/image/upload/bromance-blog/medium-d6d0d422c94a)*It is what it is.*### [Did you know that people only scan 20% of the content on your website or social media site?](https://writemysite.co.uk/13-copywriting-statistics-for-your-business/) And most people leave only after reading the headline.

This happens more times than we would like to accept and there is no denying it. It is a result of the general attitude of filtering and only clicking on things we can relate to. This is where copywriters save the day. By having in-depth knowledge and research skills, good copywriters are able to connect brands with their target demographics. Companies are more than willing to spend millions of dollars on advertisements.**Copy is a tool that uses policies such as inclusion, emotion, advocacy, and influence to connect with the audience.**Today, it has become a need for almost anyone even remotely interested in creating an online presence to ensure their content is on-point. Whether it is a small business or a Fortune 500 company — copywriting has become a requisite.

So, whether you should or should not invest in a copywriter ultimately depends on you and your needs. But there is no doubt that a good copy can do wonders and here are some examples to prove it.
