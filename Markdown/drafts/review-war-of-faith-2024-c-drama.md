This review is short and spoiler-free.

I watched this drama back in 2024 and it is one of the best dramas I have watched. I watched this drama just after giving my judiciary examination and it was such an inspirational watch. It felt like taking a vacation. So here are a few things I liked the most about this drama.

## A No-BS Show

Everything about this drama had a purpose, I was constantly on my toes.

## The Characters

Let’s start with**Wang Yibo.**After Untamed, I always felt that the characters he played didn’t do justice to his acting skills. Plus, there was a disconnect from his end too. However, with this drama, it feels like something changed in him. (You will get what I am saying after watching the first episode, just trust me on this.)

The next character that just stole my heart every time he appeared on screen was**Wang Yang.**Sadly, this is the first time I saw any of his work, a crime, I know. His character was quite complex but he depicted each developing emotion quite well. He indeed acted like a big brother, constantly trying to protect his loved ones and struggling with his own ideals.

Then we have our third lead**Li Qin.**She played Wang Yang’s younger sister. She is such a good character, doing everything in her power to make sure she does the right thing for the future. A fighter, in a true sense.

Here are my honourable mentions. Each of these characters did such an amazing job that I sobbed, guys!
